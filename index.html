Research Interests

My research interests are broadly two-fold but falls under the umbrella of electromagnetics for spacecraft vehicles and aerospace applications. Before I outline these, I wish to express my reasoning for my inclination for aerospace applications. My biggest reason is my fascination and irresistable urge to spend the rest of my life (and not just career) in exploring earth science and space by designing and building optimised technologies for it. But another, equally important motivation is my belief that in the process of building technologies for a multi-disciplinary field like space technology provides an ideal and fertile ground for the birth of technologies that have applications in myriad humane areas from low power adaptive circuit technologies to discovery of materials that have found breakthrough applications in medicine and building a low energy future for humanity.
